# ShopStack User Manual

Welcome to ShopStack! This guide will walk you through the basic features of the application.

## 1. Creating an Account

To get started, you need to create an account.

1.  Navigate to the website.
2.  Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button.
3.  Fill in your email address and choose a password.
4.  Click "Submit" to create your account. You will be automatically logged in.

## 2. Browsing Products

You can view all available products on the home page or by navigating to the "Products" page. You can click on any product to see more details, such as its description and price.

## 3. Adding Items to Your Cart

1.  On a product page, select the quantity you wish to purchase.
2.  Click the "Add to Cart" button.
3.  A notification will confirm that the item has been added. You can view your cart at any time by clicking the cart icon in the navigation bar.

## 4. Checking Out

1.  When you are ready to purchase, go to your cart.
2.  Review the items and quantities, then click "Checkout".
3.  Fill in your shipping information (name, address, etc.).
4.  Click "Place Order" to complete your purchase.

After placing your order, you will be taken to an order confirmation page, and a confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address.

Thank you for using ShopStack!
